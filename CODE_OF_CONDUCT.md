# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We, the contributors and maintainers of SpectraStrike Hub, are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all people, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

We are dedicated to fostering an open, respectful, and collaborative community where:
- Everyone feels valued and heard
- Diverse perspectives are celebrated
- Constructive dialogue is prioritized
- Mistakes are learning opportunities

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Assuming good intent in others' contributions
- Asking clarifying questions rather than making assumptions
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, sexual orientation, race, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Trolling, intentional provocation, or spreading misinformation
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Insults, mockery, or derogatory language
- Behavior that violates laws or regulations

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action in response to violations
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors who engage in harmful behavior

## Reporting Guidelines

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. **Opening a private issue** on GitHub (select "Report abuse")
2. **Contacting maintainers directly** at [ayushsh762@gmail.com](mailto:ayushsh762@gmail.com)

Include:
- Description of the behavior
- Context and circumstances
- Who was affected
- Any screenshots or documentation (if appropriate)
- Your contact information (optional, for follow-up)

### Confidentiality

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. We will:
- Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- Investigate fairly and promptly
- Respect the privacy of reporters and respondents
- Keep details confidential to the extent possible

### No Retaliation

**Retaliation against anyone who reports a violation is strictly prohibited.** Reports made in good faith will never negatively impact your standing in the community.

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

1. **Warning**: A private conversation about the concern
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary suspension from community participation (usually 7-30 days)
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community and all projects
4. **Legal Action**: In cases of serious violations, legal authorities may be involved

Maintainers will determine appropriate consequences based on severity, intent, and history.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Code comments and commit messages
- Project documentation
- Community spaces associated with this project
- Any representation of the project in public spaces
